Lifecycle & connection states

Nothing closes itself. A dropped connection is framed as the phone losing sight of the machine, never the session dying — and the one truly destructive act in this app is closing a session outright, so it gets the heaviest friction of anything here.

02

Closing a session vs. closing a child

Same verb, two very different weights. One kills a machine's tmux session; the other just stops showing a finished view. The friction is deliberately asymmetric.
